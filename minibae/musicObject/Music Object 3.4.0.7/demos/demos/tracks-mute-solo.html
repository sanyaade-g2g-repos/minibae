DEMO: The setTracksMute and setTracksSolo Methods

The setTracksMute and setTracksSolo methods, which are contained inside Music Object Extension - Groups, offer the ability to conveniently mute and solo multiple tracks of an RMF or MIDI file at a time, in one simple operation. This straightforward example will show how combinations of different musical parts of the song Hyperactive can easily be muted and unmuted, or soloed and unsoloed. Click on the various JavaScript statements below to see the effects they have on the music that's playing...
