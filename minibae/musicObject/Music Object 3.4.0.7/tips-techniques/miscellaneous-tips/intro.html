Introduction

In this section we provide tips that can help make your sonification life easier, ranging from very general tips to very specific JavaScript programming tips.

Of course, the more one can optimize one's code the better, because this will lead to smaller HTML pages which are quicker to load. The less cluttered your HTML pages, the better as well.
