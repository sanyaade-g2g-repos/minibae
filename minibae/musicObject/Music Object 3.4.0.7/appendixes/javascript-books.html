ISBN numbers are listed in the far right column. You can use these numbers when contacting a book retailer to see if they carry the book and what their selling price is.

  There may be some books in the list that have not yet been released. Books that are listed with a publishing date in the future are yet to be released. In some cases it is still possible to order these books in advance.
