 Sonify.org

Sonify.org is a community resource where developers share ideas and expertise for adding interactive audio to the Web, Wireless and Digital Devices as well as advancing the development of the underlying interactive audio technologies.

Sonify.org is technology-neutral and features information for sonifying content using a diverse array of technologies, including Beatnik's Music Object API, Microsoft's Windows Media Player, Macromedia's Flash Player, Apple's QuickTime Player, Java, and others. Because Beatnik's Music Object API is such a comprehensive platform for the creation of interactive audio applications and sonified content, there is a wealth of Beatnik-related information on this site, including a ton of excellent tutorials, showcase content, tips and tricks, and more.
