Introduction

The randomization of music on a Web page is a very useful means of keeping a Web site fresh and lively. Updating Web pages frequently is a big hinderance and so, any way of automating the dynamic selection and presentation of media is of great benefit to a Web site manager.

The steps in this tutorial will demonstrate various ways of randomly selecting music from a pool of content in an automatic scripted way that does not rely on confusing server-side cgi scripts.
